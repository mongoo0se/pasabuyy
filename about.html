About Pa-buy, Bes!

Pa-buy, Bes! is the campus marketplace created to serve the Eastern Samar State University (ESSU) community. We connect students, faculty, staff, campus stores, and student runners to make campus life more convenient through pabili requests, on-campus deliveries, and campus store promotions.

Who we serve

Our services are focused on ESSU campuses: students, faculty, staff, campus-based stores, and student runners seeking flexible earning opportunities.

Our values

  • Community-first
  • Safety and trust
  • Affordability
  • Support for campus micro-entrepreneurs
